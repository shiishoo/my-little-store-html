- History of Crochetique -

Crochetique started when I got really into crocheting. I learned lots of cool patterns and noticed many people around me liked crocheting too. But there weren't many stores that sold crochet stuff, so I decided to make one. As I learned more about crocheting and made lots of things, I saw that there were many others like me who loved it. I thought it would be great to have a store just for crocheters like us. So, I worked hard to make Crochetique a place where people could find all they needed for crocheting and meet others who loved it too. Now, Crochetique is a place where crocheters can get together, share ideas, and find everything they need to crochet amazing stuff. It's been a fun journey turning my hobby into a business that brings joy to others who love crocheting as much as I do.
